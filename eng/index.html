‚ö†Ô∏è If a switch flips down immediately after lifting it

It's likely there is a short circuit caused by a faulty appliance.

In that case, proceed as follows:

  1. Turn off all the switches in the apartment's electrical panel.
  2. Go to the meter room and lift both switches again (power meter and main breaker).

  5. Leave that switch off and turn the others back on.
